Format

5 teams per court · At least 4 rounds · 1 court · 1 King. Teams can only score points while on the “king” side. Top teams advance based on points.

Event Details

  • Entry Fee: $50 per team
  • Equipment: Official Wilson OPTX AVP volleyballs
  • Game Play: 3 rounds of 15 minutes each
  • Rules: Lowest scoring team eliminated after rounds 1 and 2. Final: First team to 15 points wins.
  • Prize: Cash & and prizes cards for top teams
