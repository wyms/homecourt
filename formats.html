6 Teams Format

6 rounds total, each team sits out exactly 1 round, everyone plays 5 rounds

Round 1

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
Sitting: Team 6

Round 2

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 6
Sitting: Team 5

Round 3

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 5, 6
Sitting: Team 4

Round 4

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 4, 5, 6
Sitting: Team 3

Round 5

Playing: Teams 1, 3, 4, 5, 6
Sitting: Team 2

Round 6

Playing: Teams 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
Sitting: Team 1

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 (sits round 6)
Team 2: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3, 4, 6 (sits round 5)
Team 3: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 (sits round 4)
Team 4: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4, 5, 6 (sits round 3)
Team 5: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4, 5, 6 (sits round 2)
Team 6: Plays rounds 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 (sits round 1)

7 Teams Format

7 rounds total, each team sits out exactly 2 rounds, everyone plays 5 rounds

Round 1

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
Sitting: Teams 6, 7

Round 2

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 6, 7
Sitting: Teams 4, 5

Round 3

Playing: Teams 1, 4, 5, 6, 7
Sitting: Teams 2, 3

Round 4

Playing: Teams 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
Sitting: Teams 7, 1

Round 5

Playing: Teams 2, 3, 4, 7, 1
Sitting: Teams 5, 6

Round 6

Playing: Teams 3, 5, 6, 7, 1
Sitting: Teams 2, 4

Round 7

Playing: Teams 4, 5, 6, 7, 2
Sitting: Teams 1, 3

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3, 5, 6 (sits rounds 4, 7)
Team 2: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4, 5, 7 (sits rounds 3, 6)
Team 3: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4, 6, 7 (sits rounds 3, 5)
Team 4: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4, 5, 7 (sits rounds 2, 6)
Team 5: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4, 6, 7 (sits rounds 2, 5)
Team 6: Plays rounds 2, 3, 4, 6, 7 (sits rounds 1, 5)
Team 7: Plays rounds 2, 3, 5, 6, 7 (sits rounds 1, 4)

8 Teams Format

5 rounds total using two courts simultaneously, everyone plays 5 rounds

Round 1

Court A: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4
Court B: Teams 5, 6, 7, 8

Round 2

Court A: Teams 1, 5, 2, 6
Court B: Teams 3, 7, 4, 8

Round 3

Court A: Teams 1, 7, 4, 6
Court B: Teams 2, 8, 3, 5

Round 4

Court A: Teams 1, 8, 3, 6
Court B: Teams 2, 7, 4, 5

Round 5

Court A: Teams 2, 4, 6, 8
Court B: Teams 1, 3, 5, 7

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts A, A, A, A, B)
Team 2: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts A, A, B, B, A)
Team 3: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts A, B, B, A, B)
Team 4: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts A, B, A, B, A)
Team 5: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts B, A, B, B, B)
Team 6: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts B, A, A, A, A)
Team 7: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts B, B, A, B, B)
Team 8: Plays all 5 rounds (Courts B, B, B, A, A)

Requires: Two courts running simultaneously with 4-team King of the Court format on each court.

9 Teams Format

5 rounds total using two courts simultaneously (alternating 4 and 5 teams), everyone plays 5 rounds

Round 1

Court A (4): Teams 1, 2, 3, 4
Court B (5): Teams 5, 6, 7, 8, 9

Round 2

Court A (5): Teams 1, 5, 6, 2, 3
Court B (4): Teams 7, 8, 9, 4

Round 3

Court A (4): Teams 1, 7, 8, 6
Court B (5): Teams 2, 9, 4, 5, 3

Round 4

Court A (5): Teams 1, 9, 4, 7, 2
Court B (4): Teams 8, 6, 5, 3

Round 5

Court A (4): Teams 5, 3, 1, 8
Court B (5): Teams 6, 9, 4, 7, 2

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays all 5 rounds (A4, A5, A4, A5, A4)
Team 2: Plays all 5 rounds (A4, A5, B5, A5, B5)
Team 3: Plays all 5 rounds (A4, A5, B5, B4, A4)
Team 4: Plays all 5 rounds (A4, B4, B5, A5, B5)
Team 5: Plays all 5 rounds (B5, A5, B5, B4, A4)
Team 6: Plays all 5 rounds (B5, A5, A4, B4, B5)
Team 7: Plays all 5 rounds (B5, B4, A4, A5, B5)
Team 8: Plays all 5 rounds (B5, B4, A4, B4, A4)
Team 9: Plays all 5 rounds (B5, B4, B5, A5, B5)

Requires: Two courts running simultaneously - one alternating between 4 and 5 teams, the other between 5 and 4 teams each round.
