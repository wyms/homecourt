Tournament Formats

King of the Court formats for different team counts

← Back to Rules

6 Teams Format

3 rounds total, each team sits out exactly 1 round, everyone plays 2 rounds

Round 1

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
Sitting: Team 6

Round 2

Playing: Teams 6, 1, 2, 3, 4
Sitting: Team 5

Round 3

Playing: Teams 5, 6, 1, 2, 3
Sitting: Team 4

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3
Team 2: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3
Team 3: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3
Team 4: Plays rounds 1, 2 (sits round 3)
Team 5: Plays rounds 1, 3 (sits round 2)
Team 6: Plays rounds 2, 3 (sits round 1)

7 Teams Format

5 rounds total, each team sits out exactly 2 rounds, everyone plays 3 rounds

Round 1

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
Sitting: Teams 6, 7

Round 2

Playing: Teams 6, 7, 1, 2, 3
Sitting: Teams 4, 5

Round 3

Playing: Teams 4, 5, 6, 7, 1
Sitting: Teams 2, 3

Round 4

Playing: Teams 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
Sitting: Teams 7, 1

Round 5

Playing: Teams 7, 1, 2, 3, 4
Sitting: Teams 5, 6

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays rounds 1, 2, 3 (sits rounds 4, 5)
Team 2: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4 (sits rounds 3, 5)
Team 3: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4 (sits rounds 3, 5)
Team 4: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4 (sits rounds 2, 5)
Team 5: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4 (sits rounds 2, 5)
Team 6: Plays rounds 2, 3, 4 (sits rounds 1, 5)
Team 7: Plays rounds 2, 3, 5 (sits rounds 1, 4)

8 Teams Format

5 rounds total, each team sits out exactly 2-3 rounds, everyone plays 2-3 rounds

Round 1

Playing: Teams 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
Sitting: Teams 6, 7, 8

Round 2

Playing: Teams 6, 7, 8, 1, 2
Sitting: Teams 3, 4, 5

Round 3

Playing: Teams 3, 4, 5, 6, 7
Sitting: Teams 8, 1, 2

Round 4

Playing: Teams 8, 1, 2, 3, 4
Sitting: Teams 5, 6, 7

Round 5

Playing: Teams 5, 6, 7, 8, 1
Sitting: Teams 2, 3, 4

Summary by Team:

Team 1: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4, 5 (sits round 3) - 4 rounds
Team 2: Plays rounds 1, 2, 4 (sits rounds 3, 5) - 3 rounds
Team 3: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4 (sits rounds 2, 5) - 3 rounds
Team 4: Plays rounds 1, 3, 4 (sits rounds 2, 5) - 3 rounds
Team 5: Plays rounds 1, 3, 5 (sits rounds 2, 4) - 3 rounds
Team 6: Plays rounds 2, 3, 5 (sits rounds 1, 4) - 3 rounds
Team 7: Plays rounds 2, 3, 5 (sits rounds 1, 4) - 3 rounds
Team 8: Plays rounds 2, 4, 5 (sits rounds 1, 3) - 3 rounds

Note: Team 1 plays one extra round (4 vs 3). For complete fairness, consider removing Team 1 from Round 5 and having Teams 5, 6, 7, 8, 2 play instead.

Format Notes

  • • All formats maintain the 5-team King of the Court structure for each round
  • • Teams sitting out can rest, warm up, or assist with reffing
  • • Final rankings determined by total points earned across all rounds played
  • • In case of ties, head-to-head results in shared rounds will be used as tiebreaker
